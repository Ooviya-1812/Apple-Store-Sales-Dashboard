# 🍎 Apple Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Apple sales performance and customer insights. The project provides dynamic visualizations, KPIs, and filters to support data-driven business decisions.

## 📊 Dashboard Pages
### 1. Sales Overview
- Total Revenue, Orders, Units Sold & Average Order Value
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Sales Channel
- Revenue by Product Category
- Top Products by Revenue

### 2. Customer Insights
- Total Customers, Average Rating & Revenue per Customer
- Revenue by Age Group
- Revenue by Customer Segment
- Revenue & Orders by Payment Method
- Key Customer Insights

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling

## 🚀 Key Features
- Interactive KPIs
- Dynamic slicers and filters
- Page navigation
- Business-focused visualizations
- Clean and responsive dashboard design

## 📌 Key Insights

- 👥 **Highest Revenue Age Group:** Customers aged **25–34** generate the highest revenue.
- 🎓 **Top Customer Segment:** The **Education** segment contributes the highest overall revenue.
- 💳 **Top Revenue Payment Method:** **Gift Card** is the leading payment method by revenue.
- 💻 **Best-Selling Product:** **Mac Pro (M2 Ultra)** generates the highest product revenue.
- 🏪 **Highest Revenue Sales Channel:** **Carrier Store** is the top-performing sales channel.
